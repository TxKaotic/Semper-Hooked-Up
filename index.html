Offshore Fishing Trips

Our offshore fishing trips take you into the deep waters of the Gulf of Mexico, where the big fish roam. We offer full-day and half-day trips, targeting a variety of species depending on the season and conditions.

Full Day Trips

8-10 hour adventures into deep water. Perfect for serious anglers looking to target larger pelagic species and make the most of their time on the water.

Half Day Trips

4-6 hour trips ideal for families or those new to offshore fishing. Great for targeting Red Snapper and other reef fish.

Pelagic Species Fishing

The Gulf of Mexico is home to incredible pelagic species that provide thrilling fights and delicious meals. Our offshore trips target these open-water predators.

Mahi-Mahi (Dolphinfish)

Colorful, acrobatic, and delicious! Mahi-Mahi are found around floating debris and weed lines in warmer months.

Yellowfin Tuna

The ultimate fighting fish! These powerful predators provide intense battles and premium sashimi-grade meat.

King Mackerel

Fast and aggressive, King Mackerel are perfect for anglers who love the excitement of high-speed runs and aerial displays.

Our Story

Semper Hooked Up Charter Service is a proud veteran-owned business born from a lifelong passion for the Gulf of Mexico and its incredible fishing opportunities. Founded by military veterans who understand the value of precision, dedication, and service, we've been guiding anglers to trophy fish for over two decades.

As a veteran-owned charter service, we bring the same commitment to excellence and attention to detail that served us in the military to every fishing expedition. Our love affair with the waters around Matagorda began years ago, and through countless hours on the water, we've developed an intimate understanding of the seasonal patterns, structures, and techniques that lead to successful fishing trips.

"Semper" means "always" in Latin - a testament to our military roots and our unwavering commitment to providing you with an exceptional fishing experience. We're honored to serve both fellow veterans and civilian anglers alike, bringing the same level of professionalism and dedication to every charter that we brought to our military service.
